21 Charts for World's Largest Countries

An in-depth exploration of the world's 21 most populous nations, which collectively represent over 70% of the global population. This project examines key economic, demographic, and social indicators across these influential countries.

Through a series of carefully curated visualizations, we explore various aspects of these nations - from population dynamics and economic growth to fiscal policies and monetary conditions. Each chart tells a part of the story of how these major countries shape our world.
